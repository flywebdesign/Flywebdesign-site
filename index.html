Nobody visits restaurant websites. Why would mine be different?+

We don't chase strangers — DoorDash does discovery fine. Your site is for the customers who already love you: regulars ordering pickup, families checking allergens. That's the winnable, commission-free business.

What about delivery? I don't have drivers.+

Pickup is commission-free from day one. Want direct delivery? We connect a flat-fee courier — a driver for a few dollars instead of 30% of the order. Keep the apps alongside for new customers.

I'm not techy. How much lands on me?+

Almost none. We build everything and connect your domain. Then we teach you one skill: the 30-second menu update. Rather text us to make changes? We'll make them.

What if I want to go back to my old site?+
